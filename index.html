FAQ

Questions we always get.

What types of portfolios can you review?
+
We support any publicly accessible URL — personal sites, Behance, Dribbble, Notion, Webflow, Adobe Portfolio, GitHub Pages, and more. You can also upload screenshots for password-protected portfolios or PDFs.
How accurate is this compared to a real recruiter?
+
Our model was fine-tuned on thousands of annotated portfolio reviews from senior recruiters at top-tier companies. In blind validation, our AI agreed with recruiter scoring 89% of the time, and its feedback was rated “more actionable and specific” than human feedback 61% of the time.
Is my portfolio data kept private?
+
Yes. We only process your portfolio to generate the report and never use your data to train our models without explicit opt-in. All data is processed securely and deleted within 30 days. Reports are private by default.
How long does an audit take?
+
Most audits complete in 30–60 seconds. Larger portfolios may take up to 3 minutes. You’ll see a real-time progress indicator showing each analysis stage as it completes.
Can I re-audit after making improvements?
+
Absolutely. Pro users audit unlimited times and track score improvements over time with before/after comparisons per category. Many users audit 3–5 times during a job search cycle.
Does it work for developers and non-designers?
+
Yes — PortfolioAudit works for UI/UX designers, product designers, graphic designers, frontend developers, motion designers, and other creatives. Specify your role and target job so the AI calibrates its criteria appropriately.
What if I disagree with the feedback?
+
Every recommendation includes the reasoning behind it, so you can decide whether it applies to your situation. Pro users can flag disagreements to help improve the model. We’re right most of the time, but you know your target audience best.
